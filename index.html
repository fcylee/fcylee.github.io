About Me

Flora grew up in Hong Kong and Canada, and arrived in the United Kingdom at 17 for undergraduate studies (BA Natural Sciences). Her first interdisciplinary research experience was in the lab of Prof. Christine Holt at Cambridge during her third year project, where she looked at a miRNA important in axon guidance. Moving to London, Flora was a Wellcome Trust Neuroscience PhD student at University College London and The Francis Crick Institute under the mentorship of Prof. Jernej Ule.

Flora is currently pursuing her fellowship project sponsored by Prof. Laura Andreae in the MRC Centre of Neurodevleopmental Disorders, King's College London.
