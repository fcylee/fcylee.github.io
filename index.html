I bridge RNA mechanisms and neuroscience. My work develops imaging, interactomics and computational methods — in close collaboration with scientists across disciplines — to understand how RNA–protein assemblies form, and how they control where and when RNAs are translated in neurons. Building on a background in protein–RNA condensation, CLIP technologies and RNA structure, I now study RNP assembly and synaptic local translation.

This research is funded by my Wellcome Trust Early Career Award and sponsored by the Andreae Lab.
