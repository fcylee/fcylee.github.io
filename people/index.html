About Flora

Flora grew up in Hong Kong and Canada, and came to the UK at 17 for undergraduate studies (BA Natural Sciences). Her first interdisciplinary research was in the lab of Prof. Christine Holt at Cambridge, working on a miRNA in axon guidance. She then completed a Wellcome Trust Neuroscience PhD at University College London and The Francis Crick Institute under Prof. Jernej Ule, before starting her fellowship sponsored by Prof. Laura Andreae at the MRC Centre for Neurodevelopmental Disorders, King's College London. Outside the lab she enjoys concerts, trying new restaurants, and keeping active.
