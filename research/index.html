Research Interests

My current research combines long-standing interests in RNA biology and developmental neurobiology, funded by my Wellcome Trust Early Career Award and sponsored by the Andreae Lab.

I aim to uncover the regulatory code of synapse-localised RNA metabolism, how this changes throughout neuronal development and under homeostatic mechanisms, as well as their contribution to synaptic dysfunction in disease models.
